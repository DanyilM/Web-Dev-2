What is the femur breaker?

.

.

.

An investigation into SCP-1050 reveals a distinct pattern of femur fractures among individuals who

exhibited noncompliance with established protocols. Initial reports indicate that these incidents often

occur in high-stakes scenarios where decision-making is critical. Interviews are conducted with those

affected, while incident reports are analyzed to identify the specific rules that were broken. The goal of

the investigation is to pinpoint the triggers for SCP-1050's intervention, implement preventative

measures, and emphasize the importance of following established guidelines to prevent further

occurrences. Additionally, collaborating with psychological experts is being considered to explore any

underlying factors that might lead to rule violations.
