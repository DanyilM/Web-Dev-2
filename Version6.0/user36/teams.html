A competition where top teams from each league can qualifty due to their standing to compete against other teams in Europe to win a trophy

𝑻𝒉𝒆 𝑪𝒉𝒂𝒎𝒑𝒊𝒐𝒏𝒔 𝑳𝒆𝒂𝒈𝒖𝒆: 𝑨 𝑩𝒓𝒊𝒆𝒇 𝑶𝒗𝒆𝒓𝒗𝒊𝒆𝒘

The Champions League is when teams from each league get selected if they meet the requirements. They can reach this by having a good season in their own league by being top 4 from each league or top 3 depending how big the league is. Once they get drawn into their group stages they then go against each team in their own group stage. After they face eachother in that same bracket then we look at the standings top 8 get to go to the next round automatically. Then 9 - 24 have to go through play offs to secure their spot in the round of 16. The rest of the teams will not continue since they did not do well. After the round of 16 is over it then becomes to the round of 8 then 4 and then to the last 2 teams who will face eachother to secure their victory and win their trophy for their team and for themselves.
